HOUSEFIGHTER

The Game:

Housefighter is a 2D Fighter where you fight 1v1. The playable characters are all houses that fight against eachother and hide in societies plain sight...
...like Transformers...
All the animations are hand drawn.

Characters:

You can choose your fighter from one of five different houses.
These houses are:
  • Familyhouse
  • Skyscraper
  • Caravan
  • Outhouse
  • Ostblock

Game Mechanics:

Housefighter borrows many elements from other series of the Fighting Game genre, like "Guilty Gear" among others.
All Characters have a super metre bar that fills up by attacking opponents, taking damage, blocking or moving forward.
This metre can be used for powerful, high damage super attacks.
A more technical use for the metre are "Roman Cancels". This action costs 50% metre and cancels the execution of your character's current move, changing it into neutral state. You can use this to extend combos or make moves with long endlag safer against your opponens' counter attacks.
Characters can cancel their lighter attacks into heavier attacks on hit.
Many attacks are also special cancelable through inputing a special move.
